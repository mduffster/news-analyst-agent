**What it means:** The “5‑day pause” (focused on *U.S. strikes on Iranian power plants/energy infrastructure*) is **not** translating into an overall operational lull. Instead, the conflict is continuing at high intensity, and Iranian pressure on **Gulf-state targets** is persisting—raising the risk of miscalculation dragging additional regional actors more directly into the conflict dynamic.

3) **Markets re-price whiplash: oil up again as fighting continues**
- AP reports Brent rose to about **$103.42** after having slumped the prior day, reflecting renewed concern as the war advances despite negotiation talk. **[REPORTED]** ([apnews.com](https://apnews.com/article/46bf2f12fb7f46c97dfe983f76e938f9?utm_source=openai))
